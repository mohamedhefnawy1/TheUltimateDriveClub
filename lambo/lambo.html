The Lamborghini Veneno
The Veneno is powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine that has a power output of 740 horsepower and 509 lb-ft of torque. This beast shares the same carbon fiber monocoque chassis with the iconic Aventador. The Veneno is more of a fighter jet than it is a car. Lamborghini attributes its design to the interpretation of a racing car built for the road. Only 12 units of the Veneno were made with three being coupes and nine being convertibles.
What makes it so special?
The starting price for the Veneno was $4 million upon its release in 2013. It remains one of the most expensive production cars in the world, selling in 2018 for a record $8.27 million.
